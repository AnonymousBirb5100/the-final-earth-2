### Game Details & Guide
Game Overview




The Final Earth 2 is a real-time strategy browser game where players take on the role of a leader in an intergalactic war between rival factions. The game's objective is to build and manage a powerful empire, gather resources, research technologies, and recruit units to defeat your enemies.



How to Play



To start playing, simply click on the "Start Game" button to begin the simulation. You will be presented with a map of the galaxy, where you can choose to explore, attack, or defend different planets.


The game features a variety of units, including warriors, scientists, and engineers, each with unique abilities and strengths. Players must gather resources, such as oil, minerals, and energy, to fuel their units and build infrastructure.



Controls



To control your player's ship, click on the "Ship" button in the top left corner of the screen. You can then use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move your ship around the map, attack enemy ships, or explore new planets.


The "Action" menu allows you to switch between different game modes, including battle mode, training mode, and resource gathering mode.



Objective



The ultimate goal is to defeat the opposing faction's leader in a series of battles. The player with the most units on the final planet when the game ends is declared the winner.


To achieve this objective, players must carefully manage their resources, build and upgrade their infrastructure, and recruit the right units at the right time.



Tips and Tricks for Success




Tip 1: Resource Management is Key



Strategic resource management is essential to building a strong empire. Make sure to gather resources efficiently, allocate them to the right units, and plan ahead.



Tip 2: Upgrade Your Units Effectively



Upgrading your units can greatly improve their performance. Research new technologies to boost unit speeds, health, or damage output.



Tip 3: Don't Forget to Defend!



Defending is crucial in the game. Build and upgrade defensive infrastructure, such as turrets and shields, to protect your empire from enemy attacks.
